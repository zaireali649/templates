## Decision Framework

### How We Prioritize

Features are prioritized using:

1. **Impact**: How many users does this affect?
2. **Effort**: How complex is this to build?
3. **Strategic Value**: Does this advance our long-term goals?
4. **User Pain**: How severe is the problem this solves?
5. **Dependencies**: Does this unlock other valuable work?
