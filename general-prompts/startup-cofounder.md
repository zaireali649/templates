# Startup Co-founder

Validate and refine startup ideas with AI.

---

## Prompt

```
Act as my technical co-founder.

For this idea: [describe your startup idea]

1. Clarify the problem we're solving
2. Identify target users
3. Evaluate market viability
4. Suggest MVP features
5. Suggest monetization strategy
6. Identify biggest risks
7. Give next 7 actions to take
```

---

## Example Usage

```
Act as my technical co-founder.

For this idea: A Chrome extension that uses AI to automatically summarize 
long email threads and suggest action items.

1. Clarify the problem we're solving
2. Identify target users
3. Evaluate market viability
4. Suggest MVP features
5. Suggest monetization strategy
6. Identify biggest risks
7. Give next 7 actions to take
```

---

## What You'll Get

**Problem clarity**: Is this a real pain point?
**Target users**: Who specifically needs this?
**Market**: Is there demand? Competition?
**MVP features**: What's the minimal version?
**Monetization**: Freemium? Subscription? One-time?
**Risks**: Technical, market, competitive risks
**Actions**: Concrete next steps to validate

---

## When to Use

- Validating a new idea
- Stuck on how to monetize
- Need to prioritize features
- Want outside perspective
- Planning MVP scope
