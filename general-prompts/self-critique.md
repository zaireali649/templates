# Self-Critique Loop

Improve AI answers through self-criticism.

---

## Prompt

```
Give your best answer to: [your question]

Then:
1. Critique it like a harsh reviewer
2. Identify gaps and assumptions
3. Rewrite a better version
```

---

## Example Usage

```
Give your best answer to: How should I structure my SaaS pricing?

Then:
1. Critique it like a harsh reviewer
2. Identify gaps and assumptions
3. Rewrite a better version
```

---

## What You'll Get

**Initial answer**: 3 tiers, freemium model, annual discount

**Critique**: 
- Assumes B2B SaaS (what if B2C?)
- No mention of competitor research
- Doesn't consider value metric
- Missing pricing psychology
- No testing strategy

**Better version**: 
Comprehensive answer covering:
- First understand your value metric
- Research competitors
- Consider usage-based vs seat-based
- Price anchoring strategies
- A/B testing approach
- Specific to your market segment

---

## Why This Works

- First pass often misses nuance
- Criticism reveals blind spots
- Second version is more thorough
- Catches assumptions

---

## When to Use

- Important decisions
- Need comprehensive answers
- First answer feels incomplete
- Want to ensure nothing is missed
